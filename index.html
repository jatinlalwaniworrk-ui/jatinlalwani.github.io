A study on E-commerce apparel sector with respect to millennials
With rapid changes in technology, each and every functioning business grows from strength to strength. This study analyses one of the fastest-growing sectors in e-commerce — the apparel industry — with a special focus on its most loyal consumer base: The Millennials.
E-commerce Consumer Behavior Millennials Retail Analytics
Abstract:

With rapid changes in technology, each and every functioning business grows from strength to strength. The e-commerce industry has seen exponential growth over the past decade, with the apparel sector emerging as one of the most dynamic and competitive categories. This study analyses consumer behaviour patterns, purchase decision drivers, and digital engagement habits of millennial shoppers in the e-commerce apparel space. The research uses a structured survey methodology across a sample of 200 millennial respondents, examining factors such as price sensitivity, brand loyalty, returns behaviour, and social media influence on purchase decisions. Findings suggest that convenience, competitive pricing, and peer recommendations are the primary drivers of millennial apparel purchases online. This paper contributes to the growing body of literature on digital consumer behaviour and has practical implications for e-commerce category managers and pricing strategists.
